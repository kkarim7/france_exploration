August 24th in Bordeaux, France

"Trade the bustle of Paris for the peaceful, rocky, island village of Mont St.-Michel by way of Normandy. This full-day, round-trip, guided tour onboard a coach transports you to medieval times for lunch and a tour in the UNESCO World Heritage Site. Admire the scenery, architecture, and explore the ancient hilltop eighth-century Benedictine abbey. Enjoy free time to wander the streets and gaze out over the Pacific Ocean."
